About simply.Map

simply.Map is designed to make your mapping easier. That's all, really. Add a point, polygon or polyline. Give it a name, and add data to each mapped feature with our simple interface. Nothing fancy here, just straightforward mapping. The data you create is completely yours, for personal or commercial use
